Developing KMC Group Policy Agreement

Slogan: Collaboration • Growth • Peace

1. Purpose of the Policy

This policy provides guidance for all members and leaders who are part of the group. It ensures discipline, unity, accountability, responsibility, and a respectful environment where every member can grow, learn, and support one another.

2. Nature of the Youth Group

  • This is an independent group created for growth, support, mentorship, and positive development
  • The group is not directly run by the church, but may participate in church activities only when officially invited or assigned.
  • All activities, meetings, and decisions are made within the group leadership.

3. Membership Requirements

  • Must be willing to work together with others as a team.
  • Must follow all guidelines in this policy.
  • Must show respect towards members and leaders.
  • Must commit to positive behaviour inside and outside group activities.
  • Must participate actively in programs, meetings, and events.
  • Must be willing to pay for the registration fee

4. Discipline Guidelines

  • No violence, disrespect, bullying, or abusive behaviour.
  • Members must handle disagreements respectfully.
  • No gossip or causing division.
  • Romantic relationships must not affect unity, respect, or activities.
  • Alcohol, drugs, and reckless behaviour are not allowed during any group-related activity.
  • Discipline issues will be handled privately by group leaders through dialogue and correction.

5. Attendance

  • Members should attend group meetings, activities, and events consistently.
  • Missing more than 3 meetings in a row without informing a leader will lead to follow-up.
  • Members must arrive on time for activities unless they have a valid reason.
  • Participation in committees, teams, or special roles requires extra commitment.

6. Communication Rules

  • Communication must be respectful at all times.
  • Official announcements will be shared through the group`s official platforms (WhatsApp, meetings, etc).
  • Members must inform a leader early if they cannot attend a planned activity.
  • No sharing group conflicts or private issues outside the group.

7. Behaviour Standards

  • All members should behave responsibly and maturely during any group activity.
  • Dressing should be decent and appropriate for the activity taking place.
  • Members should avoid unnecessary noise, disruptions, or negative behaviour during meetings.
  • Conflicts should be resolved respectfully, preferably with a leader present.
  • Members must treat every person with dignity, regardless of background.

8. Responsibilities of Members

  • Attend meetings and participate fully in activities
  • Support one another and maintain unity.
  • Respect all leaders and fellow members.
  • Follow instructions and contribute positively to the group.
  • Represent the group well both inside and outside official activities.
  • Keep group spaces clean, organized and safe.

9. Responsibilities of group Leaders

  • Lead with respect, maturity, and fairness.
  • Plan, organize, and communicate activities clearly.
  • Provide support, guidance, and mentorship to members.
  • Solve conflicts fairly and confidentially.
  • Ensure the group remains safe, disciplined, and productive.
  • Make decisions that benefit the whole group.
  • Maintain transparency and accountability.

10. Interaction With the Church

  • The group may participate in church programs only when officially requested or approved.
  • Members should maintain respect when representing the group in any church-related setting.
  • All church-related participation will be organized through group leaders.

1.1 Policy Enforcement

All members must follow this policy. Violations may lead to counselling, warnings, suspension from activities, or other actions decided by leaders. The goal of discipline is learning, correction, and restoration.

1.2 Declaration

Being part of the youth group means you agree to respect this policy, support group goals, and contribute positively to the team.
